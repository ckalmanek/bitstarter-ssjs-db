Chuck Kalmanek

CEO & FOUNDER

Chuck co-founded CrowdForce. He was Head of Research at AT&T Labs, leading a group that did large-scale data analytics and created tools to help AT&T provide best-in-class operations and customer service. His group developed AT&T Watson, a speech recognition and natural language platform. Chuck graduated from Cornell University with a B.S. in Applied Physics and from New York University with an M.S. in Computer Science. Chuck is an instrument rated private pilot.
